# Before & After — English Examples

Side-by-side comparisons showing AI-generated content transformed into natural, human writing.

---

## Example 1: Blog Post Opening

### Before (AI)

> In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, businesses must leverage cutting-edge technologies to stay competitive. It is important to note that artificial intelligence has become a pivotal tool for organizations seeking to streamline their operations and enhance customer experiences. Moreover, the transformative potential of AI cannot be understated — it serves as a catalyst for innovation across virtually every industry.

**Flags:** "In today's" opener (×1), "leverage" (×1), "cutting-edge" (×1), "it is important to note" (×1), "pivotal" (×1), "streamline" (×1), "enhance" (×1), "Moreover" (×1), "transformative" (×1), "serves as" (×1), em dash (×1), "virtually" (×1)

**Human score: 18/100**

### After (Human Touch)

> Last quarter, we cut our invoice processing time from 6 hours to 40 minutes. Not with a massive IT overhaul — with one AI workflow our operations lead built in an afternoon. That's the thing about AI in 2026: the wins aren't in boardroom strategy decks. They're in the small, specific problems someone decides to fix on a Tuesday.

**Changes:** Replaced abstract opening with concrete anecdote. Removed all AI-tell vocabulary. Added specificity (numbers, timeframe). Used conversational tone with a personality-driven ending.

**Human score: 89/100**

## Example 2: Product Description

### Before (AI)

> Our comprehensive platform offers a seamless, end-to-end solution that empowers teams to collaborate more effectively. With robust analytics and innovative features, organizations can unlock unprecedented insights and drive meaningful results. The holistic approach ensures that every stakeholder benefits from the multifaceted capabilities our tool provides.

**Flags:** "comprehensive" (×1), "seamless" (×1), "empowers" (×1), "robust" (×1), "innovative" (×1), "unprecedented" (×1), "holistic" (×1), "multifaceted" (×1), "stakeholder" (×1), vague claims with no specifics

**Human score: 12/100**

### After (Human Touch)

> Teams using our platform ship reports in half the time. The dashboard shows you exactly where projects stall — who's waiting on what, and for how long. Your designers see design metrics. Your engineers see build health. Everyone sees what matters to them, without digging through someone else's view.

**Changes:** Replaced buzzwords with concrete outcomes. Showed what the product does instead of claiming attributes. Used second person ("you," "your") for directness. Broke the paragraph into specific, scannable claims.

**Human score: 82/100**

## Example 3: Company About Page

### Before (AI)

> Founded in 2020, we are a dynamic, innovative company at the forefront of digital transformation. Our dedicated team of experts is passionate about delivering exceptional solutions that exceed expectations. We believe in fostering a culture of excellence, collaboration, and continuous improvement. Our mission is to empower businesses to thrive in the digital age.

**Flags:** "dynamic" (×1), "innovative" (×1), "forefront" (×1), "dedicated team of experts" (cliché), "passionate" (overused), "exceptional" (×1), "exceed expectations" (cliché), "fostering" (×1), "culture of excellence" (cliché), "empower" (×1), "thrive in the digital age" (×1)

**Human score: 9/100**

### After (Human Touch)

> We started in 2020 with a website builder and two founders who couldn't agree on a logo. Four years later, we're 30 people building tools that help small businesses sell online without hiring a developer. We're not changing the world. We're making it slightly less annoying to run a shop on the internet.

**Changes:** Replaced generic mission-speak with specific, honest storytelling. Added humor and self-deprecation. Used concrete details (team size, what they actually build). The personality is the differentiator.

**Human score: 91/100**

## Example 4: Email Newsletter

### Before (AI)

> We are thrilled to announce the launch of our latest feature! This groundbreaking addition to our platform will revolutionize the way you manage your workflow. We've been working tirelessly to ensure this update delivers unparalleled value to our users. Delve into the details below to discover how this can transform your daily operations.

**Flags:** "thrilled to announce" (×1), "groundbreaking" (×1), "revolutionize" (×1), "tirelessly" (filler), "unparalleled" (×1), "Delve" (×1), "transform" (×1), exclamation mark in first sentence

**Human score: 15/100**

### After (Human Touch)

> You can now schedule reports to send automatically. Set it once — pick the recipients, the frequency, and the format — and stop copy-pasting data into emails every Monday morning. We built this because three of you asked for it in the same week. Here's how it works.

**Changes:** Led with what the feature does, not how excited the company is. Gave the user's perspective ("stop copy-pasting"). Added the human story behind the feature. Ended with a direct transition, not a flowery invitation.

**Human score: 88/100**

## Example 5: LinkedIn Post

### Before (AI)

> 🚀 Excited to share that our team has achieved remarkable results this quarter! 📈 Through innovative strategies and seamless collaboration, we've managed to drive significant growth across all key metrics. It's truly a testament to the dedication and hard work of every team member. The journey continues! 💪 #Leadership #Innovation #Growth

**Flags:** Emoji overuse (×4), "Excited to share" (chatbot artifact), "remarkable" (×1), "innovative" (×1), "seamless" (×1), "significant" (vague), "testament to" (×1), "The journey continues" (cliché), hashtag stuffing

**Human score: 11/100**

### After (Human Touch)

> Our Q1 revenue was up 34% and I almost didn't post about it because I wasn't sure it would hold. It held. Three things that actually moved the needle: we stopped doing weekly all-hands (gave everyone 2 hours back), we let the support team set their own SLAs (they picked harder targets than we would have), and we killed a product line that was profitable but distracting. Sometimes growth is about what you stop doing.

**Changes:** Led with a specific number. Added vulnerability ("almost didn't post"). Listed three concrete actions instead of vague "strategies." Ended with an insight, not a platitude. No emojis, no hashtags.

**Human score: 93/100**
